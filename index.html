About Us

Who Am I?

Smile Foundation, an NGO for poor child education, is an NGO in India directly benefitting over 15,00,000 children and their families every year, through more than 400 live welfare projects on education, healthcare, livelihood and women empowerment, in over 2000 remote villages and slums across 25 states of India. Education is both the means as well as the end to a better life: the means because it empowers an individual to earn his/her livelihood and the end because it increases one's awareness on a range of issues – from healthcare to appropriate social behaviour to understanding one's rights – and in the process help him/her evolve as a better citizen.

About Us

ABOUT PREDULIVE EDUTECH FOUNDATION?

Predulive Edutech Foundation’s primary mission is to inspire, aware, educate & employ by driving large-scale job creation through entrepreneurship, innovation andskills development. The youth, especially, those living in the rural areas are unaware of the new job opportunities. The lack of appropriate training and no proper formal education in small towns and cities are also some of the reasons for unemployment. So that Predulive provide an opportunity to discover their capabilities an encouraging environment to grow through various training & workshops in the field such as electronics, telecom, agriculture, Aviation, BFSI retail, healthcare, tourism & hospitality. With a lot of employers complaining about the lack of requisite skills in new entrants, Predulive aim is to offer skill training to the youth and make them industry ready. When it is hard to afford necessities like food, shelter, and clothing; education becomes a luxury that the poor give up. Illiteracy then leads to further poverty. UNESCO declared that the Indian Education system is behind by 50 years for the 2030 Sustainable Development goals. Even after development and successful implementation of Education models around the world, Indian education relies heavily on traditional teaching methods involving rote memorization and similar testing methods. Predulive Edutech Foundation, through the implementation of project based learning and hands-on activities, hopes to bring back the joy in learning. With the use of innovative approaches to teaching, they hope to ignite the spark of curiosity in children and gently chisel them to take form befitting the world standard. Predulive always try to Collaborate with multiple startups of different -2 industries like Drones, Medical & Agriculture etc. for building a good ecosystem for our youth & our unemployed workers.
