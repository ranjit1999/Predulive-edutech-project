About Us

Who Am I?

Smile Foundation, an NGO for poor child education, is an NGO in India directly benefitting over 15,00,000 children and their families every year, through more than 400 live welfare projects on education, healthcare, livelihood and women empowerment, in over 2000 remote villages and slums across 25 states of India. Education is both the means as well as the end to a better life: the means because it empowers an individual to earn his/her livelihood and the end because it increases one's awareness on a range of issues – from healthcare to appropriate social behaviour to understanding one's rights – and in the process help him/her evolve as a better citizen.

How We Started?

Athria Edutech Foundation came to be with the realisation of the standard of education in Indian schools. UNESCO declared that the Indian Education system is behind by 50 years for the 2030. Sustainable Development Goals. Some researchers say it could take up to 6 generations for India to achieve world standard education. One of the primary reasons stated is the quality of teaching found in Indian schools, especially Government schools which is below par. Even after development and successfu implementation of Education models around the world, Indian education relies heavily on traditional teaching methods involving rote memorization and similar testing methods. Many top ranking countries in the world have adopted methods that involve teaching through hands-on activities, Application oriented studies and project based learning These methods all have roots in technology based learning.
